What You Want to Know About The Night of Power "Lailat Al-Qadr" as a Muslim

in this article I'm going to be talking about The Night of Power "Lailat Al-Qadr" and what you want to know about this blessed night inshallah.

  1. What is Lailat Al-Qadr?

What is Lailat Al-Qadr?

Lailat Al-Qadr is the night which Allah 'سبحانه وتعالى' revealed the Qur'an to his Prophet Muhammed 'عليه الصلاة والسلام'.

The Time and Sign of Lailat Al-Qadr

Lailat Al-Qadr starts in the last 10 days of Ramadan, and the sign of it appears in the next morning that comes it, accoarding to this Hadith narrated in Sahih Muslim (762a) [Ubayy said: By Allah I there is no god but He, that (Lailat-ul-Qadr) is in Ramadhan (He swore without reservation: ) By Allah, I know the night; it is the night on which the Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) commanded us to pray. It is that which precedes the morning of twenty-seventy and its indication is that the sun rises bright on that day without rays.]

The Virtues of Lailat Al-Qadr

This blessed night has virtues every muslim should know to encourage them to take advantage of this blessed night.

The Holy Qur'an Was Revealed in it

Allah 'سبحانه وتعالى' revealed the Qur'an to his Prophet Muhammed 'عليه الصلاة والسلام' in that night. Allah 'سبحانه وتعالى' said in the Qur'an: [Indeed, We revealed it (the Quran) on the Night of Honour.] [97:1]

The Rulings of The Entire Year are Implemented in it

Allah 'سبحانه وتعالى' implement the rulings of the entire year in this night. Allah 'تبارك وتعالى' said in the Qur'an: [Every wise matter is distributed therein. As a command from Us; indeed We are those who send.] [44:4-5]

It is One of Blessed Nights of The Year

Allah 'سبحانه وتعالى' said in the Qur'an: [Certainly, We sent it down on a blessed night, ˹for˺ We always warn.] [44:3]

Worshipping Allah in This Night is Better Than Worshipping for a Thousand Months

Allah 'سبحانه وتعالى' said in the Qur'an: [The Night of Honour is better than a thousand months.] [97:3]
